BRAZIL METHODOLOGY & PLATFORM INCLUSIONS Figures represent an exclusive Cross-Screen Attention Index generated via ESHAP analysis tailored to the Latin American media landscape. It models sovereign, platform-specific measurement streams into a singular, logic-enforced zero-sum marketplace budget across linear screens, mobile devices, and computers. The baseline models total available time boundaries utilizing the Instituto Brasileiro de Geografia e Estatistica (IBGE) census records and regional consumption diaries. Platform totals represent unified corporate parent structures (including Globoplay and ViX). Concurrent multi-screening duplication and passive device use discounted. The data applies country-specific demographic matrices to filter out the heavy 55+ segment, cleanly exposing the high-intensity attention shares of the active workforce pool and youth brackets.
